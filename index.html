11 Bizzare American Restaurants
  1. The Safe House – Milwaukee, WI

    2. The Safe house is a spy agent themed restaurant located in Miliwakee, WI. This restaurant will help you embrace your inner James Bond. The Safe House will have you comple a mission , and only then will they unlock their doors. After completing your mission relax and try some of their dishes like, “spycialties” like “Agent Provocateur Steak Sandwich” and “Cuban Missile Crisis.”

    Check out the restaurant website here.

  2. Tommy Gun’s Garage – Chicago

    3. Tommy’s Gun’s Garage is a 1920s themed restaurant in Chicago. This restaurant is not only taking you back to the 1920s, but you may also enjoy an interactive dinner show. Tommy’s Gun’s Garage goes above and beyond to make their guest as if they are in the twenties. The menu, “Big Bill” Thompson, “Don’t Call Me Chicken” and “The Kingpin” before watching a music and comedy show. Actors perform musical selections from Duke Ellington, George Gershwin and Cole Porter.
